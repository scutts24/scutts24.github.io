I mixed two unrelated images to make a meme. The kitten in the background is a disabled foster cat named Tater Tot who became a popular figure on platforms like Twitter and Instagram for a brief time before passing away. The rest of the image is a well-known frame from 'The End of Evangelion' in the 'Neon Genesis Evangelion' Japanese anime series. Tater Tot is edited as a large figure in the background of the image because, in the original frame, the two characters on the beach are looking at the face of a third character who, in summary, has been transformed into a giant version of themself and hovers over a destroyed Earth. The edit is supposed to represent Tater Tot's fame, the sadness at his passing, and the strong positive impact his story left on people.

RIP Tater Tot
