While shuffling around in the darkness, you get startled by a sudden noise nearby and whip around to try to discover the source. In so doing, you stumble and reach out for something to help steady yourself. Your right hand slams down onto an unseen surface, but something sharp stabs into your palm before dislodging itself and clattering onto the floor.

You mutter a curse and bring your hand closer to your face to assess the damage. There is a jagged gash across your palm, glinting and barely visible in the candlelight. The surface you grabbed onto turns out to be a very cluttered desk and you realize you must have slammed your palm onto whatever used to sit atop the surface of the corner closest to you.

You gently feel around on the floor to find the source of your injury and discover a large pair of scissors. You try your best to slow the bleeding while calling out for her in the darkness.
