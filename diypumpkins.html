This is the result of an assignment called DIY "photoshop" from fall 2023, in which I was required to code simple drawing and stamping tools in p5js. I chose to make mine based on pumpkin carving because I was randomly looking back on the past month and feeling a little sad that I didn't have time to carve pumpkins for Halloween.

To select a tool, press a number between [1] and [0] or the letter [G] then draw or stamp on the canvas with left click and mouse. Press [X] to clear your drawing.
