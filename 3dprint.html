| Fall 2023: 3D printing projects |

└---------------------------------┘

The first project I 3D printed for this class was the Springtrap figure I made in Minecraft (shown in the previous page). I still have to find a way to fill in the gaps in the plastic, since the acrylic paint I tried was not enough to do so on its own.

The second print was for my final project, which is a sardine I modeled in Blender and split in half before printing multiple copies. The halves will be glued together, painted, and potentially put into an empty sardine can. This will be done to represent how trash is having a negative impact on marine life and introducing microplastics into the seafood we consume.
